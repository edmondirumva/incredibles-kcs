Kigali Christian School Programs

KCS is adequately equipped with spacious classrooms, a computer laboratory, library, science laboratory, art room, and several recreational fields (playground, football pitch, and basketball and volleyball courts.) KCS currently has Nursery, Primary, and Secondary School Sections. As a Christian school, Christian values and principles are integrated throughout the entire school, in each classroom, and is evident in each subject matter covered. Staff are all committed followers of Jesus Christ striving to accomplish KCs mission

Through discipline and excellence, we commit ourselves to provide quality education by ensuring that our students are equipped with the knowledge and skills necessary to become Christ-like servants of God and the community.
